Services

Discover & Understand

Through interviews with people and other research methods, I discover and seek a deeper understanding of problems to generate insights that will drive ideation. I also work closely with stakeholders to know the market and identify opportunities of high potential.

Experiment & Validate

From sketching wireframes to crafting hi-fidelity mockups, I make ideas visible for validation or presentation. I create concepts, test hypotheses, and design processes for scale and accessibility.

Analyse & Improve

I assess the impact of the design with feedback and through analysing behavioural data, reflect on the good and the bad, and make improvements to the design.
